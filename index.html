Welcome to cdn.docbook.org

This site hosts the DocBook stylesheets and schema files.

DocBook schema

  • Online content is in schema/.

DocBook XSL (XSLT 1.0 stylesheets)

The DocBook XSL stylesheets come in two flavors, one to process DocBook 5.x and later (DocBook in a namespace) and one to process DocBook 4.x and earlier (DocBook not in a namespace).

For DocBook 5.x

  • Packages are on GitHub.
  • Online content is in release/xsl/.

For DocBook 4.x and earlier

  • Packages are on GitHub.
  • Online content is in release/xsl-nons/.

DocBook XSLT 2.0 (XSLT 2.0 stylesheets)

  • Packages are on GitHub or in Maven.
  • Online content is in release/xsl20/ (sic).
  • Online resources (CSS, JavaScript, etc.) are in resources/.

DocBook xslTNG (XSLT 3.0 stylesheets)

  • Packages are on GitHub or in Maven.
  • Online content is in release/xsltng/.
