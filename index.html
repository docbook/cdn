Welcome to docbook.github.com

Last update: 11 September 2011

This site hosts the “official” releases of the DocBook XSLT 2.0 stylesheets as well as the JavaScript, CSS, and other ancillary files referenced by the DocBook stylesheets.

You're welcome to leave the references in place, or copy these assets to your local server. You can download them by cloning the repository.

I haven't worked out a policy for dealing with versioning the ancillary files, so all that's available right now is “latest”. YMMV.
